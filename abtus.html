How it began

From a dream to
your daily cup.

The story begins in 2018, in the Tondo neighborhood of Asuncion — where our very first shop opened its doors. What started as the dream of a corporate employee slowly became a beloved reality. We set out to provide guests with freshly ground coffee, tea, hearty meals, and sweets, all crafted with care.

We work hard to uphold the greatest standards of quality and service, fusing innovation with tradition to create one-of-a-kind experiences. Every coffee cup we offer has a backstory — and so does Leo's Café.

Where we are

Find us in
the heart of Tondo.

Located at 1198 Asuncion Street, Tondo, Manila, Leo's Café has been a neighborhood staple since August 9, 2018. We serve a full range of hot and cold beverages — from classic coffee to smoothies — alongside food favorites like burgers, carbonara, and desserts.

Open every day, 2:00 PM – 10:00 PM. Come as you are; stay as long as you like.

The person behind it all

Meet Sir Leo.

Leo's Café is owned and run by Sir Leo Richmond Arguelles — the corporate employee who turned his passion for coffee into a community cornerstone. His personal recommendation? The Salted Caramel Latte, for its irresistible balance of sweet and salty that keeps customers coming back.

Every policy, every recipe, every warm greeting — it all flows from Sir Leo's commitment to making Leo's Café a place where people feel at home.
